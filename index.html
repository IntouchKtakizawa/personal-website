01 about

Kenta Takizawa is a Year 10 student at SPIP (Bangkok) with a passion for engineering, music, and theatre production. He currently serves as Tech Director for his school's theatre program, leading production for Mean Girls after previous roles as Projection Technician (Aladdin) and House Usher (Hakuna Matata).

A multi-instrumentalist who plays guitar, bass, and drums, Kenta also likes to freestyle rap in his spare time. He's building toward a future in biomedical and aerospace engineering, with interests spanning coding and filmmaking. He is half Thai, half Japanese.
